Intro

This is a documentation of my drum machine project! I am trying to make a device with similar controls to the tr-808, but with a lot less features. I also want the drum machines to mimic old synth drums and krautrock, especially Kraftwerk. I am aiming to have four drum sounds: kick, snare, open hi-hat, and closed hi-hat.

Sound Inspiration:

Computer World by Kraftwerk


Rotopops by LOOK MUM NO COMPUTER and Hainbach

Galaxis by Kosmicher Laufer
